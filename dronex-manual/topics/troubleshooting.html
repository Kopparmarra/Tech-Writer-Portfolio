Troubleshooting

This section helps you solve common problems with the Dronex device.

Below is a list of common issues and their respective troubleshooting guides:

  • The drone does not power on

    If your drone does not power on, ensure the following:

    • Check if the battery is fully charged.
    • Ensure the battery is properly connected to the drone.
    • Inspect the Power button for any physical damage.
    Important:
    Contact customer support if the issue persists after performing these checks.
