Battery Safety

This section outlines important safety information when using the Dronex.

Follow these instructions if you encounter a leaking battery:

  1. Immediately stop using the Dronex and turn it off.
  2. Wear protective gloves to avoid direct contact with the leaking material.
  3. Carefully remove the battery from the device, avoiding any spilled liquid.
  4. Place the leaking battery in a sealed plastic bag to contain the leakage.
  5. Dispose of the battery according to local hazardous waste disposal regulations.
  6. Thoroughly clean any surfaces that came into contact with the leaking material using a damp cloth and mild detergent.
Warning:
Do not attempt to use or charge a leaking battery. Contact Dronex support for further assistance.
